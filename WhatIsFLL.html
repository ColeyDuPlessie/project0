In FLL, kids compete in local and state, and even global competitions! FLL has three aspects, the:
Robot game, which is the most iconic part. It consists of building and programming a LEGO Mindstorms robot do do missions, such as pushing things.
Project, basically a science fair type project on a specific theme, this year the theme is into orbit.
Core values are principles that teams must abide by, such as kids do the work.
All these will be judged at the competition in late november or early december.
